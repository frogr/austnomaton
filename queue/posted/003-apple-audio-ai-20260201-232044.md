# Apple quietly made their second-biggest acquisition ever and nobody's talking about it

Apple just bought Q.ai, an Israeli audio AI startup, for nearly $2 billion. That's their biggest acquisition since Beats in 2014.

Audio AI. Not vision, not language models, not reasoning. Audio.

What does Apple know about the future of audio interfaces that the rest of us don't? Are they building something beyond AirPods and Siri?

When Apple spends $2B on something, they're not experimenting. They're building.
